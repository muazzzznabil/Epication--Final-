3 Day Lavertezzo Itinerary

Day-1

Morning
  • Breakfast at local cafe

    • We will start our day with local Swiss breakfast at local cafe near our staying place.

Afternoon
  • Valle Verzasca Exploration

    • We go by car to the centre of the Verzasca Valley, a charming town renowned for its crystal-clear river and double-arch stone bridge. Spend some time strolling around the village and admiring the scenic surroundings..

Evening
  • Dinner at Swiss-Italian restaurant

    • Fill our belly with dinner in one of the local restaurants, savouring Swiss-Italian food.


Day-2

Morning
  • Breakfast at local cafe

    • We will start our day with local Swiss breakfast at local cafe near our staying place.

Afternoon
  • Visit Ascona

    • We will go there by travel bus. After arrive, everyone can explore the place at their own pace to enjoy the pictureque sceene with beautiful Lake Magiore

Evening
  • Dinner at Asconak

    • After strolling, we will dinner at local restaurant at Ascona to dine local cuisisne.


Day-3

Morning
  • Breakfast at local cafe nearest Locarno

    • We will start our day with local Swiss breakfast at local cafe near Locarno because this place always full of people from evening to night.

Afternoon
  • Explore Sacro Monte e Santorio Madonna del Sasso

    • This is place that must visit when travel to Lavertezzo because it have a lot of historical story with a beautiful architecture

    Evening
  • Dinner in the Orselina

    • For supper, we pick a restaurant in the historic district near Locarno. A spectacular dining experience may be had by visiting one of the many restaurants that provide an authentic taste of Lavertezzo
