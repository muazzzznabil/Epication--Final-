    1. Each participant must complete 2 doses of vaccination.

    1. PCR/RTK test should be done for official confirmation.

    4. The trip will follow the itinerary that has been set for other participants who are healthy and not infected with Covid-19.

  3. Captain's Responsibilities
    1. Helping and organizing the affairs of participants infected with Covid-19 (booking accommodation, eating and drinking and other appropriate things).
    2. The Captain's Assistant will return to Malaysia with participants who are healthy and not infected with Covid-19 after the end of the trip.
    3. The captain will return to Malaysia late to be prepared in case of a case of Covid-19 among the participants.
